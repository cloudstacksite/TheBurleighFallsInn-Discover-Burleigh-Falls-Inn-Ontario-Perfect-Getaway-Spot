Explore the Surrounding Attractions Near Burleigh Falls Inn

One of the advantages of staying at Burleigh Falls Inn is its proximity to various natural and cultural attractions, giving guests plenty of options to explore beyond the resort.

1. Outdoor Adventures

Kawartha Lakes offers a myriad of outdoor activities, appealing to guests of all ages and interests.

  • Hiking Trails: Discover scenic trails that wind through lush forests and offer panoramic views of the surrounding lakes.
  • Fishing and Boating: Enjoy a day on the water with fishing, canoeing, or kayaking, with rental options nearby.
  • Winter Sports: In winter, the area transforms into a snowy playground with opportunities for snowshoeing, cross-country skiing, and ice fishing.

2. Local Towns and Cultural Sites

If you’re interested in exploring nearby towns or learning more about local history, there are several cultural and heritage sites within a short drive.

  • Peterborough: Known for its vibrant arts scene, Peterborough has galleries, museums, and unique shopping experiences.
  • Kawartha Settlers' Village: Step back in time and see what life was like in the 19th century at this living history village.

3. Day Trips and Scenic Drives

Plan a day trip to see more of Ontario’s beautiful countryside, or take a leisurely drive through the charming towns around Kawartha Lakes for a deeper sense of the region's character.
